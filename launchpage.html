What is NSS?

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a Government of India programme under the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports. It develops student youth through community service with the motto “Not Me, But You.”

Key objectives include: understanding the community and oneself, identifying needs & problems, involving students in problem-solving, developing leadership & democratic attitudes, and gaining skills in mobilising community participation. (Source: official NSS objectives)

Source refs: NSS intro and objectives pages.

Our Focus @ GEHU Bhimtal

  • Cleanliness & environment (plantation, plastic-free drives)
  • Health (blood donation, basic check-ups, first-aid awareness)
  • Digital literacy for nearby communities
  • Road safety & civic awareness campaigns
  • Special camps & collaborative events with district admins
Growing every month with new partners & volunteers.
